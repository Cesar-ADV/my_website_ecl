Select Your Training Path

Train your ear to understand military English in various environments and speeds.

Level 1: Foundations

Score: 0 - 30

Start with clear, slow-paced audio. Focus on identifying key words, numbers, times, and basic questions in quiet environments.

Level 2: Operational

Score: 40 - 70

Move to natural conversational speed. Practice with standard American accents and scenarios involving motor pools, flight lines, and mild background noise.

Level 3: Mission Ready

Score: 71+ (Target: 80%+)

Tackle the hardest ECL audio. Train with simulated radio transmissions, heavy background noise (combat/flight line), and implicit meanings.
