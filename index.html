The ECL Examination: What You Need to Know

Official regulations translated into your training strategy.

🎯 The Mission

The ECL is the official gateway for International Military Students (IMS) and non-native US personnel to attend US-sponsored training, exercises, and commissioning. It measures strict English operational readiness.

How this Hub helps: We immerse you in the specific military logistics and technical vocabulary required to prove your readiness.

⏱️ Test Format

Expect 100 multiple-choice questions purely focused on Listening and Reading (no speaking/writing). The standard test takes 75 minutes, while the DLI adaptive version (CAT) takes 25. Both score from 0 to 100.

How this Hub helps: Our timed Mock Tests recreate this exact pressure, training your stamina for both standard and fast-paced adaptations.

⚠️ Strict Regulations

To prevent score manipulation, regulations dictate you can only take the ECL once a month. Furthermore, your passing score expires after 180 days because language skills deteriorate without practice.

How this Hub helps: Make your one monthly shot count! Consistent practice with our daily Grammar and Skills modules keeps your proficiency sharp.

🛡️ Official Administration

Unlike standard English tests, the ECL is highly classified. It is exclusively controlled by US Government Test Control Officers (TCOs) globally and shares a design foundation with the ALCPT.

How this Hub helps: We provide ALCPT-equivalent practice environments so you are completely familiar with the mechanics before facing the official TCO.
