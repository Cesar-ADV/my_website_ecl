Examination Overview

The ECL consists of 100 multiple-choice items. It assesses listening and reading comprehension in both general and military contexts.

  • Part I (Listening): 66 items (approx. 30-35 min).
  • Part II (Reading): 34 items (30 min) covering grammar and vocabulary.

ECL Grammar

Review key structures: Conditionals, Passive Voice, and Reported Speech.

Military Oriented Vocabulary

Technical terms, ranks, logistics, and service-related terminology.

Functional Language

Giving orders, requesting permission, and office-related communication.

Listening Skills

Auditory training featuring various accents and background noise environments.

Reading Comprehension

Fast-paced text analysis and technical paragraph comprehension.

Test your knowledge with 100-item timed practice tests.
